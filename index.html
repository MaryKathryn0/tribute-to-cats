The McMeowser Family

In order from left to right, clockwise

Where are they meow? A timeline.

  • Dudley Kins McMeowser - Dudley was just a wee boy when this photograph was taken. Through out gradeschool and highschool Dudley explored the world of dance and later grew up to a renowned choreographer of participation dances. He would later be known as the father of the Meow-corana- a spin off of the macarena that included his family cats.

  • Envey Lope McMeowser - If you are in the flashlight business you most likely heard of Envey, the McMeowser family matriarch. Born in Alaska, Envey dreamed of lighting up her world and in 1912 she purchased an old stapler factory and turned into McMeowser Lights & Lasers, the worlds leader in flashlight & cat laser pointers.

  • Angelina Margot McMeowser - Born Angelina Margot Cattington in London, England, Angelina first met Bull McMeowser(pictured to the right) at Texas A&M University where they both attended college. They married shortly after graduation and several years later had their first child, Dudley. It was said tht Angelina was very close with her mother in law Envey which sometimes caused family squabbles.

  • Bull Shizzle McMeowser - Bull McMeowser was the first born son of Envey and Bob and was a professional pie baker who worked with Pies-R-Us, as the CPO (Chief Pie Officer). Bull achieved success with well-established companies such as American Pie Company & Pies2Go, to name a few; utilizing his unique Genetically Modified Pie-Fruit Methodology (GMPFM); that he developed when at Texas A&M University . After spending more than 30 years in the pie industry, Bull was awarded the Golden Pie Award, a lifetime acheivement.

  • Bob Katz-McMeowser - Bob Katz was born in Bellvue, Washington, where his father Ali was a biologist and his mother Kallieco raised the children. When he was seven years old, his family moved to Anchorage, Alaska, where he grew up and later became a pilot and met Envey. Bob fell in love with Envey and her entrepreneurial spirit but declined her wish to take her maiden name and instead compromised by using a hyphen and adding McMeowser.

  • Jen-Anne Tawnique McMeowser - Jen-Anne was originally poised to take over the McMeowser family flashlight business after she graduated from the prestigous Stanford University. However Jen-Anne got in with the wrong crowd and ended up a member of the Cats & Claws gang. Jen-Anne worked her way up the gang to become its leader and made a signifcant fortune in pirated tuna. Though she posed in this family portrait there was always a family rift regarding her mothers close relationship with her sister-in-law Angelina.

  • Iwilla Growl McMeowser - Featured in this picture in her stroller, Iwilla, was a rambuctious tot and the second most known of the McMeowser family. Immediately after graduating highschool Iwilla decided to travel the world and hired a fully staffed sailboat as her vehicle to adventure. Iwilla was not only the first McMeowser to visit over 94 countries but also followed her mother as an entreprenuer and started the first line of preserved-prehistoric cheese shop, Say Cheese. Say Cheese first store opened at the Salamanca Market in Hobart, Tasmania in the country of Australia. 47 Say Cheese stores still operate today and also has a widely popular e-commerce customer base.
